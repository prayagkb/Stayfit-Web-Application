App Description

Definition

Apps are small, specialized programs (applications) designed to be user-friendly on a PC or any other device. Fitness apps are designed specifically to assist with exercise, running , body temperature and other types of physical training or related fitness topics.

Purpose

The purpose of a fitness app is to provide the user with instructions and examples of one or more types of exercise, physical activity or some other fitness topic. Many fitness apps are available to download from the internet. Some are used to count calories, others record statistics on workouts or collect data on walks, runs, and bike rides. Out fitness app connects the user to a systematic UI to help with areas of concerns when using a specific fitness routine or just generally with workouts. Further, Out fitness app provides a coordinated trackers which helps the user in well being i.e.Mood Tracker
